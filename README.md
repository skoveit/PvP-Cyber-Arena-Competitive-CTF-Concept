## Overview
PvP Cyber Arena is a **conceptual project** for a competitive cybersecurity platform that transforms traditional Capture The Flag (CTF) into a **player-vs-player experience** inspired by strategy games and anime-style battles.

Instead of solving static challenges, players actively **build, defend, and attack real systems** in live matches, combining offensive and defensive cybersecurity skills in a gamified, competitive environment.

This repository currently serves as a **vision and design reference**, not an implementation.

---

## Core Idea
- Players or teams compete in **1v1 or Team vs Team** matches.
- Each player/team owns a **customizable system (digital base)**:
  - Running services
  - Defensive configurations
  - Intentional, controlled vulnerabilities
- Matches are based on **Attack & Defense**:
  - Exploit opponentsâ€™ systems
  - Defend your own infrastructure
- Progression system inspired by games:
  - Levels
  - Upgrades
  - Ranks & seasons
- Strong **gamification layer** with anime-style visuals and feedback.

---

## What Makes It Different
- Player vs Player hacking (not player vs challenge)
- Live attack and defense at the same time
- Long-term progression instead of one-off challenges
- Game-first experience with real cybersecurity skills underneath

---

## Goals
- Make cybersecurity learning more engaging and competitive
- Encourage real red-team and blue-team thinking
- Build a platform people return to daily, not just practice once
